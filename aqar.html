AQAR Report

The Annual Quality Assurance Report (AQAR) is a comprehensive document submitted to the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) annually. It reflects the institution's continuous efforts toward academic and administrative excellence by showcasing significant activities, initiatives, and improvements implemented during the academic year. The AQAR serves as a key instrument in monitoring progress and ensuring quality in all areas, including teaching, learning, research, and community engagement.
